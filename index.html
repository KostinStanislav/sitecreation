Who is responsible for the safety of flight?

Pilot

An operator of the aircraft who controll and monitor all actions inside the cabin permitted by controller. Responsible for the safety of 1 aircraft,which is controlled by him at the moment,and for all persons on board.

Controller

A person who controll air traffic,gives permition how to fly,where to fly and what buttons pilot have to press in the cabin. Responsible for the safety of all aircrafts, all persons on board,and all pilots flying at the same time in his responsibility area.

Not everyone can be an ATC!

To be an ATC you should fit some characteristics

Also you have to:

Study hard in university or academy of aviation
Have perfect health to pass medical examination and get apropriate certificate.(you shoul be ready to pass it once in a 2 years)
Pass Exams
Pass FEAST test (First European ATC separation test)
Finish additional education in special training center
Get practice at your working place,learn and remember tons of work documents

Done!You may apply all neccesary documents to get ATC licence,and pass final test :)
